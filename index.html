Follow My 3 Step Training Technique

Step 1: Establish a Strong Body Position

When starting out it is crucial to not form bad habits and feel safe and comfortable on the bike. We'll focus on building the foundation that every fast rider needs.

Step 2: Move More Forward on the Bike

This progresses you to ride more like the pros. Moving forward on the bike provides more control and allows you to go faster.

Step 3: Start Training Fitness with Motos

Once technique is solid, we add fitness training through structured motos. If you make the same mistake twice, we identify and fix the technique problem at its root.

Important Note: I do not train strictly motos or "speed" because you don't go faster around a corner by entering faster - you go faster by having more control and putting the bike where it needs to be. This is achieved through technique training and why I can be faster than kids with better bikes, who don't go to school, and have a track on their property.
